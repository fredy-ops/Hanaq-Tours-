The Hanaq Difference

We are not just a travel agency; we are your local guides to the magic of the Andes.

Our Roots in the Highlands

Hanaq Tours, named for the Quechua word meaning 'Heavenly' or 'Upper World,' was founded by a team of certified local guides with a deep passion for Peruvian history and ecology. Our mission is to offer tours that go beyond the typical path, ensuring authenticity, sustainability, and respect for the local communities.

We focus on high-altitude experiences, providing specialized training and equipment to ensure your safety and comfort while exploring the majestic Andean peaks.
